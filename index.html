Game rules:

Bulls and Cows is a logic game.

The computer generates four digit number and the player tries to gues it.

The numbers have to be with four digits and the digits must be all different.

At each turn the player tries a four digit number, and the computer says how close it is to the answer by giving:

  • The number of Bulls - digits correct in the right position.
  • The number of Cows - digits correct but in the wrong position.
Learn more about the game

Example:

If the number is 1234 and your guess is 1243.

1, 2 are bulls and 3,4 are cows. Basically in this case you have 2 bulls and 2 cows. Your goal - 4 Bulls !
